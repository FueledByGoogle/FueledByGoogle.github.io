S2Mosaic - Cloud detection and satellite mosaicing [On Github]

We compiled several weeks of Sentinel2 satellite data into a cloud-free, histogram-corrected multi-band map. With our pre-processed data we were able to achieve significantly better results than if raw Sentinel2 L2A data was fed directly to S2Cloudless. The most notable differences was the ability to detect wispy clouds, while S2Cloudless without any pre-processing can pick out thick white clouds with a dark land/water mass underneath, it is unable to detect wispy clouds if no pre-processing is applied to the raw data.
